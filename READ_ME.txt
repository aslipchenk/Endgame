install:
    1. open your terminal in the game directory/repo and print "make"
    2. print "./endgame"
    ...
    3. profit

_Manual_
game has two mods:
    1) battle where you need kill the enemies and don`t allow them go to the Unit,
        if they reach it - you lost one of your lifes, if enemies touch you - you die
    2) runner mod, where you need run right as long as possible,
        falls or touchsng with enemies - destroy your lifes
    also every mod has his own multiplayer where you can play with your friend with one keyboard

control menu:
    1. buttons "1", "2", "3" for choosing game mod in any menu window
    2. button "ESC" for exit from main menu out of the game or any window in the game in second menu
    3. button "q" for exit from second menu in the main menu or exit from any other window out of the game

control gameplay:
    button "p" - pause in the game
    button "ESC" - exit from pause or exit in the menu without saving result

    1st player:
        buttons "w", "a", "d", for control player moves
        button "SPACE" for shooting
    2nd player:
        buttons "up", "left", "right", for control player moves
        button "0" for shooting

other - is on your exploring, good luck
